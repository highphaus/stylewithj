                                            Uf plant  
                                        
                                        
                                              A UF plant (Ultrafiltration plant) is a water treatment system that uses ultrafiltration membranes to remove suspended solids, bacteria, viruses, and other particles from water. UF is a pressure-driven membrane filtration process that filters water through a semi-permeable membrane with pore sizes ranging from 0.01 to 0.1 microns.
